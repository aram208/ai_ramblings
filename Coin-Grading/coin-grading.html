Coin grading research notes¶

A coin's grade is generally determined by five criteria: strike, preservation, luster, color, and attractiveness. In this research we will try to propose a model for grading automation of modern coins by focusing on luster and color.

Below is an example of two American Silver Eagle 2018 coins graded 69 and 70.

69 70

Observations:

- Both histograms show the same pattern with two strongly pronounced maximums and a rugged tail on the right
- Absolute max for the grade 70 is much higher than that of the grade 69 
- Absolute max values peak between buckets 100 and 150
- Both plots show rugged behavior around bucket 250 (light colors) with grade 69 having much higher variance
